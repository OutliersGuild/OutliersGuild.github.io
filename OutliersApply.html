Before you apply:

Before choosing to apply, please make sure that our schedule works for you and that you read through the FAQ section to make sure that Outliers is the place for you. We do not review poorly written applications, so complete all fields a let us know that you want to be a part of the team. Once you submit, officers will review your application and add you on Bnet to ask any further question or to offer you a trial. If we are interested in having you trial, you should hear back from an officer within 3 days of submitting the application.

After you apply:

Any person recruited into the guild to raid will undergo a 2 week trial period. During this time we will still cover flasks, food, etc. After 2 weeks, officers will decide whether you convert to "raider", extend your trial, or convert you to a member.
